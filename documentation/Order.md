# Order Types

In the stock market mod, you can place different types of orders to buy or sell items on the global market. Understanding how each type works helps you make better trading decisions.

---

## Buy/Sell Market Order

A **Market Order** is the simplest type of order.

- **Buy Market Order**: Instantly buys the desired amount of an item at the lowest available price.
- **Sell Market Order**: Instantly sells the item at the highest price currently offered by a buyer.

## Buy/Sell Limit Order

A **Limit Order** lets you set the exact price you're willing to buy or sell at.

- **Buy Limit Order**: You specify the maximum price you're willing to pay. The order will only be filled if a seller offers the item at that price or lower.
- **Sell Limit Order**: You specify the minimum price you're willing to accept. The order will only be filled if a buyer is willing to pay that price or more.
