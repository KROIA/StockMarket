# v2.0.4 — In Development

## Villager Trading

| Tag | Change |
|---:|---|
| ![feature] | <details><summary>Stock-market-driven villager trade repricing</summary><br>When enabled, villager and wandering-trader offers for market-listed items are rewritten at interaction time: emerald costs are replaced by the configured trading-currency item, priced from the live stock market with configurable margins (villager buys below market, sells above market). Items without a market keep their normal vanilla emerald trades — offers convert automatically when a market is created and revert when it is deleted.<br><br><ul><li>New <code>VillagerTrading</code> group in <code>settings.json</code>: <code>ENABLED</code> (default <code>false</code>), <code>PRICE_REFRESH_INTERVAL_MINUTES</code> (default <code>20</code> ≈ one Minecraft day), <code>VILLAGER_BUY_MARGIN</code> (default <code>0.8</code>), <code>VILLAGER_SELL_MARGIN</code> (default <code>1.2</code>).</li><li><b>Master-authoritative:</b> only the master server reads the settings; it broadcasts the price table (including the currency item) to all slave servers on a new server-to-server packet, so enabling it on the master enables it everywhere.</li><li>Original emerald offers are stored in world data, so disabling the feature restores every villager's original trades. Item ingredient slots (e.g. book + emeralds for enchanted books) are preserved — only the emerald part becomes currency.</li></ul></details> |
| ![feature] | <details><summary>Pay merchant money costs with any combination of denominations</summary><br>Offers priced in BankSystem money are matched by <b>value</b>, not by exact note type: a 20.00 cost can be paid with 1×20, 2×10, 4×5, or even a single 50 note, split across both payment slots.<br><br><ul><li>Exact combinations are consumed cleanly; overpayment returns exact change (fewest notes) into the payment slots, the inventory, or dropped at the player's feet — no value is ever lost, even when shift-clicking through a whole stack of large notes.</li><li>Clicking an offer row auto-fills the payment slots with money from the inventory, even when the exact denomination shown on the offer is not owned.</li><li>Only genuine BankSystem money is matched by value — renamed/modified notes, other mods' currencies, and non-money offers keep untouched vanilla behavior.</li></ul></details> |
